# References and historical sources

This repository documentation summarizes standard material from numerical-analysis, differential-equation, and computational-physics literature. Key historical references include:

1. Leonhard Euler, *Institutionum Calculi Integralis*, Vol. I, 1768. Euler's work is historically associated with the method now called the Euler method.
2. Carl Runge, "Über die numerische Auflösung von Differentialgleichungen," *Mathematische Annalen*, 1895.
3. Wilhelm Kutta, "Beitrag zur näherungsweisen Integration totaler Differentialgleichungen," *Zeitschrift für Mathematik und Physik*, 1901.
4. Erwin Fehlberg, *Low-Order Classical Runge-Kutta Formulas with Stepsize Control and Their Application to Some Heat Transfer Problems*, NASA TR R-315, 1969.
5. J. R. Dormand and P. J. Prince, "A family of embedded Runge-Kutta formulae," *Journal of Computational and Applied Mathematics*, 1980.
6. Charles F. Curtiss and Joseph O. Hirschfelder, "Integration of stiff equations," *PNAS*, 1952.
7. C. William Gear, work on numerical integration of ordinary differential equations and stiff multistep methods, 1960s.
8. Loup Verlet, "Computer Experiments on Classical Fluids. I. Thermodynamical Properties of Lennard-Jones Molecules," *Physical Review*, 1967.
9. Nathan M. Newmark, "A Method of Computation for Structural Dynamics," *Journal of the Engineering Mechanics Division*, ASCE, 1959.
10. Edward N. Lorenz, "Deterministic Nonperiodic Flow," *Journal of the Atmospheric Sciences*, 1963.
11. Balthasar van der Pol, papers on relaxation oscillations and nonlinear oscillators, especially the 1926 formulation of the dimensionless oscillator.
12. H. H. Robertson, stiff chemical kinetics test problem, 1966.
13. Johannes Kepler, *Astronomia Nova* and Kepler's laws of planetary motion; Isaac Newton, *Philosophiæ Naturalis Principia Mathematica*, 1687.
14. Ernst Hairer, Syvert P. Nørsett, and Gerhard Wanner, *Solving Ordinary Differential Equations I: Nonstiff Problems*.
15. Ernst Hairer and Gerhard Wanner, *Solving Ordinary Differential Equations II: Stiff and Differential-Algebraic Problems*.
16. John C. Butcher, *Numerical Methods for Ordinary Differential Equations*.
17. Uri M. Ascher and Linda R. Petzold, *Computer Methods for Ordinary Differential Equations and Differential-Algebraic Equations*.
18. E. Hairer, C. Lubich, and G. Wanner, *Geometric Numerical Integration*.
19. H. H. Rosenbrock, "Some general implicit processes for the numerical solution of differential equations," *The Computer Journal*, 1963.
20. Haruo Yoshida, "Construction of higher order symplectic integrators," *Physics Letters A*, 1990.
21. Gilbert Strang, "On the construction and comparison of difference schemes," *SIAM Journal on Numerical Analysis*, 1968.
22. O. Gonzalez, "Time integration and discrete Hamiltonian systems," *Journal of Nonlinear Science*, 1996.

The Markdown files in `docs/methods/` and `docs/benchmarks/` are concise technical notes, not primary-source historical scholarship. For publication-grade history, verify details against the original papers and books above.
